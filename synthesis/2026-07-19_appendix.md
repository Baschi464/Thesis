# Whole-thesis appendix — raw data tables + supplementary figures

This is the appendix at the very end of the thesis (after the GP chapter,
before the bibliography). It uses `\appendix`, so its sections render as
Appendix A / B / C. It exists to hold the raw numbers and extra photos that
would clutter the main chapters but are worth preserving for reproducibility.

**Appendix A — FEM data** (from `ComsolResults.xlsx`): three tables backing the
COMSOL study of the inchworm actuator — wall-thickness sweep (@3 kPa),
fiber-spacing sweep (@2 kPa), and the design comparison (baseline vs
EcoFlex 00-50 walls vs radial fibers, for 1 and 2 chambers @3 kPa). The
main-text §II now cites these tables. Note: the radial-fibers study is
included here (the earlier "it's wrong" was retracted).

**Appendix B — actuator characterization** (from `3x Actuator.xlsx`): the raw
pressure–angle(–tip-displacement) data behind the bending experiments. Three
tables:
- Full-scale segment (L = 60 mm), a full inflation→deflation loop, peaking at
  181.4° @ 14.8 kPa. This is the data plotted in the §IV
  `inflationvsdeflation` figure, and it's why §IV now says 14.8 kPa (not the
  paper's 15.5) — 14.8 is what the data and the existing lizard §
  (bending-characterization-miniaturized) both report.
- Miniaturized segment without central fiber (→172.6° @ 16.4 kPa).
- Miniaturized segment with central fiber (→178.8° @ 15.0 kPa).
The last two underpin the lizard chapter's miniaturization comparison.

**Appendix C — supplementary figures**: fiber-wrapping photo, the soft-hinge
at rest vs bent (showing the passive foot rotation), and the full 15-frame
floor-to-wall transition sequence (the main-text §VIII shows only the
condensed `TransitionGait` strip and points here for the full sequence).
